Help & Notes

How to Use

  • Select a provider and model, then wait for it to load
  • Click Record or press the Fn/Globe key
  • Speak naturally — transcription happens in real time
  • Click Stop or release the hotkey to finish
  • With Paste mode, text is inserted into your frontmost app

Providers

  • Whisper — OpenAI Whisper via Candle (Metal GPU accelerated)
  • Moonshine — Lightweight, fast model for quick transcription
  • CoreML Whisper — Apple CoreML optimized Whisper

Pipeline

  • Audio captured from mic via push-to-talk
  • VAD splits audio into utterance chunks
  • Resampled to 16kHz mono PCM
  • Whisper inference produces raw transcription
  • Optional LLM formatter cleans up text with guardrails
  • Final text output to clipboard or frontmost app
